HIDDEN

Hidden is a battle royale game where players start naked and collect coins to buy clothes with varying values. The goal is to have the highest clothing value at the end and reach the cinema. Players use toy guns with slippery ammo to stun opponents and steal coins. They can buy clothes from vending machines and use parkour skills to navigate the city. Players must also avoid patrolling drones and maintain their clothing value in this unique and fast-paced game.

About Me

Hi! My name is Omar Al-Guthmy, and I'm a passionate game designer originally from Kenya, currently residing in the UK. I recently completed my undergraduate studies in BSc Game Design, where I had the opportunity to work on various exciting projects. The projects I've been involved in range from university assignments to personal and GameJam creations. Throughout my projects, I took on the role of a technical designer, where I had the chance to showcase my skills in programming and design. I thoroughly enjoy being creative, and my proficiency in 3D modeling has been an asset, making communication and collaboration within teams smoother and more efficient. I take great pride in my work and always strive to exceed expectations. I believe in continuous learning and welcome new challenges to further enhance my skills. If you have any questions or would like to know more about my projects, please feel free to reach out to me. I'm looking forward to connecting with you :)
